# Equity Valuation – Credicorp Ltd. (BAP)

## Project Overview

This project aims to estimate the intrinsic value of Credicorp Ltd. using fundamental valuation techniques including Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) analysis and comparable company analysis.

---

## Company Overview

Credicorp Ltd. (NYSE: BAP) is the largest financial services holding company in Peru and one of the leading financial institutions in Latin America. Through its subsidiaries, the company provides commercial and retail banking, insurance, pension fund management, investment banking, wealth management and asset management services.

The company's main subsidiary is Banco de Crédito del Perú (BCP), the largest bank in Peru by assets and loans. Credicorp also operates through Pacifico Seguros, Prima AFP, Credicorp Capital and Mibanco, allowing it to serve individuals, corporations and small businesses across multiple financial segments.

Because of its diversified business model and strong market position, Credicorp is an interesting case study for equity valuation. This project estimates the intrinsic value of the company using fundamental analysis and compares it with the current market price.
